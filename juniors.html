The Minnesota Juniors Box League (MJBL) is a juniors box lacrosse league for players ages 16–22, organized and hosted by the Inver Grove Isotopes at Veterans Memorial Community Center — tryouts, games, and a championship, all in one summer.

About the League

Where Isotopes
Fans Start.

The Minnesota Juniors Box League (MJBL) is a league built to help juniors players ages 16–22 get better — more reps, more game experience, and a competitive environment to grow in. Tryouts kick off the season, followed by a run of games, a tournament trip to Vermont, and a championship to close things out.

Coaching involves current and former Senior players focused on fundamentals, floor awareness, and a love for the box game. New players and families are always welcome — no box experience required to start.
