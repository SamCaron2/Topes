Who We Are

Built for Box.
Built for
Inver Grove.

The Inver Grove Isotopes are a Senior A men's box lacrosse team competing in the North American Box Lacrosse League (NABLL) and the Interstate Box Lacrosse Association (IBLA). We play fast, physical, competitive lacrosse and we've built a roster of players who hold each other to a high standard — on and off the floor.

We call Veterans Memorial Community Center home — a tight, fast box where crowd noise carries and every game matters. The club also organizes and runs the Isotopes Juniors league, giving the next generation of box lacrosse players a place to play right here in Inver Grove Heights.

We're always looking for competitive, coachable players who want to play at a high level. The Isotopes run a tight, well-organized program — if you're serious about box lacrosse, we want to hear from you.

18+ years old and eligible for Senior A competition
All positions considered — attack, defense, midfield, goalie
