Who We Are

Built for Box.
Built for
Inver Grove.

The Inver Grove Isotopes are a Senior men's box lacrosse team competing in the North American Box Lacrosse League (NABLL). We play fast, physical, competitive lacrosse and we've built a roster of players who hold each other to a high standard — on and off the floor.

We call Veterans Memorial Community Center home — a tight, fast box where crowd noise carries and every game matters. The club also organizes and runs the Minnesota Juniors Box League (MJBL), giving junior players a place to compete and get better right here in Inver Grove Heights.

Open Tryouts

Think You Can
Keep Up?

We're always looking for competitive, coachable players who want to play at a high level. The Isotopes run a tight, well-organized program — if you're serious about box lacrosse, we want to hear from you.

18+ years old and eligible for Senior competition
Serious about box lacrosse and ready to compete — you want to be here and want to play
All positions considered — forward, defense, transition, and goalie
Box lacrosse experience preferred, strong field players welcome
Ability to commit to the full season schedule
Own equipment required (sticks, gloves, helmet, pads)

Interested in the Minnesota Juniors Box League for your kid instead? Sign up here or visit the Juniors page.
